Program technology

  1. Be responsible for the research and development of internal information related systems, and have good development habits;

  2. Identify needs through good business understanding ability, and participate in the internal technical system and system planning of the Department;

  3. Work with front-end students to develop project interface documents and specifications.

Job requirements:

  1. Computer / software related majors;

  2. Have good java programming foundation and coding specification, and be familiar with common data structures and algorithms;

  3. Proficient in common technical frameworks and basic principles of implementation, such as springboot and springcloud;

  4. Proficient in MySQL and mongodb database, familiar with redis or Memcache caching system;

  5. Familiar with common attack forms and solutions in Web environment;

  6. Have good ability to analyze and solve problems, and be able to undertake complex system development independently;

  7. Good communication skills, clear thinking and good at thinking;

  8. Have a strong sense of responsibility and team spirit, and actively integrate into the team.

Animation designer / special effects designer

Art performance

Job description

Job responsibilities:

  1. Skin binding of 3D game characters; Making animation resources related to in-game scenes / 3D characters; (animation direction)

  2. Make special effects related to characters / scenes in 3D games; Make 2D special effects related to UI. (special effect direction)

Job requirements:

  1. Art related majors;

  2. Have good aesthetic ability and love games;

  3. Proficient in using Maya / 3dsmax / AE / PS and other production software;

  4. Strong learning ability and perseverance, hard-working, strict requirements and pursuit of professional level;

  5. Strong expression and communication skills, willing to share.

Work requirements:

Game, animation related design or drawing works. For still frame works, please submit the common picture format (jpg / TGA / TIFF / PNG / BMP). The picture compression rate should not be too large to avoid affecting the picture quality. Please do not submit PSD project files with layers and too large volume; Please provide screenshots of 3D works, and please do not submit the original engineering documents; For animation and sequence frame works, please submit the common video format (MP4 / AVI / WMV / mov). The video file can be properly compressed and output on the premise of ensuring the picture quality, so as to avoid inconvenient transmission caused by too large submitted file.
